# Bacon

★★★★★

Serves: 4
Time: 20 minutes

## Ingredients

* 12 slices bacon

## Instructions

1. Adjust oven rack to middle position and heat oven to 400 degrees.

2. Arrange bacon slices on a wire rack in a rimmed baking sheet. Roast for 16-17 minutes.
