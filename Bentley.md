# Bentley

Non-alcholholic version of [[Bajan Rum Punch]].

## Ingredients

- 1 cup fresh squeezed lime juice
- 2 cups Bajan home made sugar syrup made with raw cane sugar dissolved into water until it is a thick brown syrup.
- 4 cups water
- A cherry (optional)

## Instructions

1. In a pitcher, combine the lime juice, syrup, and water and stir well.
2. Pour into glasses filled with ice and add a cherry.
