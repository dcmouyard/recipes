# Aunt Julie’s Lemon Chicken

★☆

Serves:
Time:

Notes about this recipe.

## Ingredients

### Sauce

* 2 tablespoons soy sauce
* ½ teaspoon ground pepper
* ¼ cup (vegetable?) oil
* ¾ cup fresh lemon juice
* 2 tablespoons grated lemon peel
* 1 clove garlic, minced

### Chicken

* 4 chicken breasts, slice to ¼ (inch?)
* ½ cup flour
* 1 teaspoon salt
* 1 teaspoon ground pepper
* 1 tablespoon paprika
* ½ cup butter, melted

## Instructions

1. Combine 2 tablespoons soy sauce, ½ teaspoon ground pepper, ¼ cup oil, ¾ cup lemon juice, 2 tablespoons grated lemon peel, and 1 clove minced garlic. Mix and refrigerate.

2. Preheat oven to 400℉.

3. Combine ½ cup flour, 1 teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon ground pepper, and 1 tablespoon paprika in a shallow dish. Coat chicken with flour mixture. Arrange chicken in a shallow pan. Brush with ½ cup melted butter.

4. Bake uncovered for 20 minutes.

5. Turn chicken. Pour sauce mixture over chicken and continue baking 20 more minutes.

6. Best served with rice and broccoli.
