# Hummingbird

Serves: 2

## Ingredients

* 2 ounces dark rum
* 2 ounces coffee liqueur
* 2 ounces cream
* 4 large strawberries
* 1 banana
* 3 cups crushed ice

## Instructions

1. Add everything but ice to blender and mix.

2. Add ice and blend until smooth and frothy.
