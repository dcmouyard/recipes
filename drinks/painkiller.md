# Painkiller

Serves: 1

## Ingredients

* 2-4 ounces dark rum
* 4 ounces pineapple juice
* 1 ounce orange juice
* 1 ounce cream of coconut
* freshly ground ground nutmeg

## Instructions

1. Combine all but nutmeg in a shaker. Pour into a glass of ice. Add freshly ground nutmeg on top.
