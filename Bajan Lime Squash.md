# Bajan Lime Squash

## Ingredients

- 1 cup fresh squeezed lime juice
- 2 cups Bajan home made sugar syrup made with raw cane sugar dissolved into water until it is a thick brown syrup.
- 4 cups soda water
- Grated nutmeg
- A slice of lemon or lime (optional)

## Instructions

1. In a pitcher, combine the lime juice, syrup, and soda water and stir well.
2. Pour into glasses filled with ice, and sprinkle the nutmeg over the top.Â 
3. Add a slice of lemon or lime.
